Jaggery Market

Jaggery is a natural, traditional product of sugarcane. Its color varies from honey brown to dark yellow. The Anakapalli region of Andhra Pradesh has a large number of jaggery manufacturing units, mostly located in rural areas, where processors follow conventional methods to produce it. Jaggery is the main income generator in most of the rural areas in and around the Anakapalli region.
